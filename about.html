My Story

I am a data analyst, full stack developer and content creator who is always wanting to learn about how things work and why they were created in the first place. Coming from a nearly decade-long journey in the journalism field, I feel that the transition to data analytics and full stack development is a natural one - both require extreme curiousity, a willingness to learn new skills and an ability to be a creative and adaptive problem solver.


Although I have extreme Midwest Pride - being born and raised in Minnesota and now residing in South Minneapolis - I have a place in my heart for both the West and the East Coast as well as South East Asia. My want for knowledge along with an extreme wanderlust has put me in some fun situations. Want to know more? Let's connect.

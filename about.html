My story

I am a data analyst, developer and content creator who is always wanting to learn about how things work and why they were created in the first place. Coming from a nearly decade-long journey in the journalism field, I believe the transition to the data analyst and developer role is a natural one - both require extreme curiosity, a willingness to learn new skills and an ability to be a creative and adaptive problem solver.

The best compliment I have ever recieved was when a friend said, "When you think of an idea - you just do it, even if you know you might fail." I prefer to struggle through problems and take each failure as an opportunity to learn. I will always try because I'm an optimist to a fault.

Although I have extreme Midwest pride - I was born and raised in Minnesota and now reside in South Minneapolis - I have a place in my heart for both the West and East Coast. My want for knowledge and extreme wanderlust has brought me to some remarkable places. Want to know more? Let's Connect.

My story

I am a data analyst, developer and content creator who is always wanting to learn about how things work, why they were created and how they might be made more efficient. Coming from a nearly decade-long journey in the journalism field, I believe the transition to the data analyst and developer role is a natural one - both require extreme curiosity, a willingness to learn new skills and an ability to be a creative and adaptive problem solver.

I prefer to struggle through problems and see failure only as an opportunity to learn. I pride myself on having a wide range of knowledge as well as an ability to focus completely on a subject.

Growing up in a rural town in central Minnesota, living in South Minneapolis and being lucky enough to travel the world, I like to think I am able to see problems from a different perspective. My want for knowledge and extreme wanderlust has brought me to some remarkable places. Want to know more? Let's Connect.
